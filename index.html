Longitude Photography

Business Strategy

Your goal is to make people feel a sense of adventure and awe, compelling them to buy a print of the beauty they see.

Reels & TikTok

Use short video montages of a recent shoot. The purpose here is **discovery** and **audience growth**. Use music suggestions and emotional captions.

Feed Posts

Post your best finished landscape photos. This builds your **portfolio** and nurtures your **existing community**. Use the app to generate a caption with a technical tone to attract other photographers, or a grow audience tone to gain new followers.

Stories

This is for **engagement** and **authenticity**. Share behind-the-scenes looks at a shoot or run polls to ask for feedback. This builds connection and keeps you top-of-mind.

Figure and Form Photography

Business Strategy

Your goal is to empower potential clients, make them feel comfortable and beautiful, and show them the value of your services.

Reels & TikTok

Create short videos from a past shoot (with client permission). Their purpose is to showcase the positive energy and empowering **experience** of a session. Use the app's music suggestions to set the right mood.

Feed Posts

Post a mix of your strongest portfolio shots and testimonials from happy clients. This builds **trust** and **reassurance**. Use a grow engagement tone from the app to ask questions and invite comments.

Stories

Stories are key for building **rapport**. Show behind-the-scenes content of you preparing for a shoot, answer client questions in a Q&A, or share encouraging words about body positivity and confidence.
